How to Bid

  • Home
  • How to Bid
Step-01

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free RapidVenta account. Provide your name, email address, and a secure password. Once registered, our admin team will validate your account before you can start bidding.

Step-02

Browse Auctions

Explore our live auction listings. Use the search bar or filter by category, price, or closing time to find exactly what you're looking for. Each listing shows the current bid and time remaining.

Step-03

Place Your Bid

Open any auction listing and enter your bid amount — it must be higher than the current price. Watch the live countdown timer. If your bid is the highest when the auction ends, you win!

Step-04

Win & Complete Payment

If you win, you'll receive a notification immediately. Head to your dashboard to confirm the order and complete secure payment. Our team coordinates delivery once payment is confirmed.

Step-05

Track Your Orders

After payment, track your purchase from your personal dashboard under "My Orders". Our support team is available via WhatsApp if you have any questions during delivery.

Bidding Guidelines

Bid Responsibly

Only bid what you are prepared to pay. All winning bids are binding and must be completed within the payment window.

Watch the Countdown

Each auction runs until the timer hits zero. Monitor listings closely — last-second bids are common in competitive auctions.

Use Your Watchlist

Add items to your Watchlist to track them easily. You'll have quick access to all items you're interested in from your dashboard.

Secure Payments

All payments are processed securely. We support major payment methods and your financial data is always protected.

Verified Sellers

Every seller on RapidVenta is verified by our admin team. Buy with confidence knowing every listing meets our quality standards.

24/7 Support

Got a question? Our support team is available via WhatsApp around the clock to help you with any issue during the bidding process.
