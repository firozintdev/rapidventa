Step-01

Registration

Create a free account and select the Seller role during registration. Submit the required identity documents. Our admin team reviews and validates your seller account before you can list items.

Step-02

Select Platform

Once validated, access your seller dashboard. Choose a pricing structure — set a minimum public price, maximum public price, and optionally a secret reserve price to protect your bottom line.

Step-03

List Your Item

Create your auction listing with a clear title, detailed description, high-quality photos, and an accurate category. Set your start and end times to control when the auction runs live.

Step-04

Manage Your Auction

Track live bids in real time from your seller dashboard. Use our bulk CSV upload to add multiple listings at once. Monitor performance with detailed analytics on each listing.

Step-05

Get Paid

When your auction closes and a winner completes payment, the funds are processed securely. Our team coordinates the handover and keeps you updated at every stage of the transaction.

Seller Guidelines

Quality Photos

Clear, well-lit photos dramatically increase bidding activity. Upload multiple angles to give buyers a full view of your item.

Accurate Pricing

Set realistic starting prices. A low minimum attracts more bidders and often drives competitive prices higher naturally.

Detailed Descriptions

Be precise about condition, dimensions, brand, and any defects. Transparency builds trust and reduces disputes after the sale.

Bulk Upload via CSV

Selling multiple items? Use our CSV upload tool to add hundreds of listings at once — saving hours of manual data entry.

Track with Analytics

Access your seller analytics dashboard to see bid counts, total revenue, and auction performance to improve future listings.

Seller Protection

Our platform includes a secret reserve price — your auction won't close below your minimum acceptable price, protecting your investment.
